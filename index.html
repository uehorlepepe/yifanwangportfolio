About

About Me

I'm a student focused on aerospace engineering, with a strong interest in how machine learning can support better aircraft performance, more efficient operations, and smarter design decisions. My work usually lives between research, prototyping, and building things that make technical ideas feel real.

Outside of research, I like turning ideas into projects, whether that means launching an aviation-focused AI tool, building experimental RC aircraft, or expanding STEM access through Elevon EX.

Focus Machine learning for aviation efficiency, aerodynamics, and applied engineering research.
Approach Combine wind tunnel experiments, computational analysis, and practical builds to test ideas end to end.
Current Direction Expand published and in-progress aviation research while building tools that can be used outside the lab.

Aerodynamic Analysis of Conventional and Non-Traditional Paper Aircraft: Wind Tunnel and Flight Analysis

This study demonstrates that paper aircraft geometry significantly influences airflow behavior, stability, and efficiency, with small design changes leading to major differences in flight performance.
